Digital Portrait for Client
(August 2023)
This picture reimagines a classic portrait via the aesthetic prism of anime,
emphasizing the subject's eye color. Typical of anime, the eyes are large and
filled with vivid colors that enhance their expressiveness. The depth and
passion expressed in the eyes are emphasized by fine shading and highlights,
which both reflect the essence of the original portrait and lend an anime-
specific, stylized charm.
The metamorphosis incorporates characteristics of anime style, such as
dynamic compositions, clear lines, and heightened emotion. The character's
stare and the minute reflections in the eyes are only two examples of how each
element of the photograph has been painstakingly created to improve the
narrative and aesthetic effect.

AApp Used : Adobe illustration
Inspiration: Inspired from Indian pilgrims who are devoted to Lord Shiva, especially those in Banaras, serve as my source of inspiration. These
devotees travel great distances to take part in holy pilgrimages, driven by their intense spiritual devotion and dynamic traditions. Their
commitment and the bright, eye-catching clothes they wear have greatly influenced my style. Banaras provides the idea for this piece because of its
spiritual atmosphere and diverse cultural fabric. The rich colors of the pilgrims' clothing, the rhythmic chanting, and the bustling ghats combine to
create a striking scene that embodies devotion and cultural legacy.
I try to capture in my designs the earthy tones, rich textures, and elaborate patterns found in the pilgrims' clothing. The ancient designs, the
layering of fabrics, and the use of natural dyes are all strongly inspired by the aesthetic and spiritual aspects of Banaras. This city is a rich source
of inspiration due to its ageless beauty and spiritual significance, which enables me to produce designs that are not only spectacular to look at but
also rich in cultural and spiritual value.

(August 2023)
