Working method

From sample to screen.

02

Research trends, explore silhouettes, build colour stories, and translate ideas into clear concept directions.

03

Correct colour, lighting, edges, and composition. Compare against the source and resolve inconsistencies.

Organise final assets and build concise visual boards that make development reviews faster and more confident.
